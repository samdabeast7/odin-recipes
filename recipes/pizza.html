Pizza Recipe

Cheesy and Delicous pizza

Ingredients

Pizza sauce

  • 1/2 cup of water
  • 12 ounces of tomato paste
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano,crushed
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil, crushed
  • 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon onion powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper

    • Pizza Crust

  • 3 1/4 cups of all purpose flour, or more as needed
  • .25 ounces RapidRise Yeast
  • 1 tbsp sugar
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons salt
  • 1 1/3 cups very warm water
  • 1/3 cup oil

    • Toppings

  • Mozzarella cheese to taste
  • Pepperoni

    • Steps

    1. Preheat the oven to 425 degrees F and grease two large pans
    2. Make sauce: whisk all ingredients together and set aside
    3. Make crust: Combine 2 cups of flour, yeast, sugar and salt in a large bowl
    4. Add warm water and oil and mix until well blended and add small amounts of flour at a time until a ball of sticky dough forms
    5. Knead dough until it's smooth
    6. Let dough rest covered for 10 minutes
    7. Divide dough n half and pat into the prepared pizza pans
    8. Top dough with sauce cheese and pepperoni
    9. Bake in oven for 18 to 20 minutes
