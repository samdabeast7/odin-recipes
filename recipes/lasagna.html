Lasagna Recipe

Special homemade lasgana that is very meaty and it is all made from scratch and tastes beatiful

Ingredients

  • Meat: 1/2 pound ground pork and 1/2 pound lean ground beef
  • Diced Onion
  • Can of tomato sauce
  • Can of diced tomatoes
  • 2 tbsps of parsley chopped
  • One clove of garlic crushed
  • Dash of sugar
  • Dried basil, Dried Oregano, Salt, Black pepper
  • Lasgana noodles
  • Cottage, parmesan and Mozzarella cheese
  • Eggs

    • Steps

    1. Cook the meat in a skillet until browned, add the onion and continue
    2. Stir in the Canned tomato products with parsley, garlic, basil, salt, oregano and sugar
    3. Cook the noodles in lightly salted water until they are fully cooked
    4. Mix cottage cheese, Parmesan cheese, eggs, parsley, salt and pepper in a bowl
    5. Layer the lasagna according to your discretion
    6. Bake the lasagna for 50 minutes at 375 degrees
