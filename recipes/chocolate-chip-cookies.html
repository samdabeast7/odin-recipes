Chocolate Chip Cookie Recipe

These cookie are chewy beyond belief they just take you back like you are a young child again!

Ingredients

  • 1 cuup butter, softened
  • 1 cup white sugar
  • 1 cup packed brown sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 2 teaspoons hot water
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 3 cups all purpose flour
  • 2 cups semisweer chocolate chips
